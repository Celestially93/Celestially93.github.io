Story of the Week

Breastfeeding on demand may seem a rigorous job, especially when you have a pile of chores and other things to attend to, but I came to feel the joy and bond as I sit to feed my little one. It relaxes my body and my soul.

One thing I discovered upon reading an article in the web, breastfeeding produces the naturally soothing hormones oxytocin and prolactin that promote stress reduction and positive feelings in the nursing mother. Increased confidence and self-esteem. Increased calmness. Breastfed babies cry less overall, and have fewer incidences of childhood illness.-Anonynous
